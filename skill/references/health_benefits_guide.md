# Sprouts & Microgreens: Health Benefits Reference Guide

## Quick Lookup: Best Varieties by Health Goal

### Immune Support
| Variety | Form | Key Compounds | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Broccoli | Both | Sulforaphane, Vitamin C & E, beta-carotene | Top immune pick; sulforaphane activates the body's own antioxidant defenses |
| Kale | Microgreen | Vitamins A, C, K, iron | Exceptionally high Vitamin C |
| Sunflower | Microgreen | Vitamin E, selenium, zinc | Vitamin E is a potent immune modulator |
| Alfalfa | Sprout | Vitamins C & K, saponins | Gentle, beginner-friendly |
| Mustard | Microgreen | Vitamins A, C, antioxidants | Spicy flavor; strong immune boosters |

### Gut Health & Digestion
| Variety | Form | Key Compounds | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Fenugreek | Sprout | Digestive enzymes, soluble fiber | Historically used for digestive issues; aids bloating |
| Radish | Both | Amylase, digestive enzymes, fiber | Stimulates bile production; aids fat digestion |
| Pea shoots | Microgreen | Fiber, digestive enzymes, vitamins | High fiber; gentle on stomach |
| Fennel | Sprout | Anethole, volatile oils | Classic digestive herb; relieves gas and cramps |
| Mung bean | Sprout | Digestive enzymes, fiber, prebiotic | One of the easiest sprouts; enzyme-rich |
